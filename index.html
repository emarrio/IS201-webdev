Our Mission

Safe and fun swimming for every child

Pool Safety

We help students ages 3+ to become more pool safe. For beginners this can simply mean floating, getting to the side of the pool, or even learning to be comfortable in the water. For more advanced swimmers this includes knowing strokes and swimming long distances.

One on One lessons

Kids learn best when they have our full attention. We give personalized 25 minute lessons to each student, providing one on one feedback and coaching.

Fun for all!

A huge priority for us is ensuring that each student enjoys their time in the pool. We do our best to lessons engaging and enjoyable in order to create a safe and happy environment in the pool. Additionally, we offer a waterpolo clinic for older advanced swimmers- which is always a highlight of the summer!

About

Marriott family swim

  • 2005-2010

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Believe it or not, we had to learn to swim too! We all grew to love being in the water like true California kids, thanks to the loving and expert instruction from our teachers as children. We draw lots of inspiration from 'Miss Holly' to this day!

  • 2017-2021

    Waterpolo man and learning to teach

    These years mark Alden's high school water polo and swim career- he was especially good at racing Butterfly and was a top scorer on his team. This is also when Elaina and Alden began to be trained as swim teachers. It's a fun, rewarding and hard job- and we're still doing it!

  • 2019-2022

    College and missions

    In this time frame, both Alden and Elaina went on to college, teaching swim the summers in between. We've loved meeting your kids and seeing them grow!

  • This summer '23

    More lessons, and adding teacher Jane

    With our sister Jane graduating from high school this year, we are adding her to our swim team. We are currently training her in preparation and are confident that she'll be one of the best teachers in no time. She is an expert with kids, and has been an official teacher's aide in a local elementary school for class credit her senior year. We're excited for her to join us!
